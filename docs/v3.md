## End to end journey
On https://chalagente.com the landing page is concise and the look and feel is refined. It works on all breakpoints. There is a demo link, log in link, privacy policy and terms. The business goes to 'Demo' (`/demo`) and sees a prefilled example of a taco stand. They hit send on the prefilled user message, which is a voice note which says 'Bonjour, qu'est-ce que la birria ?'. The agent responds with a voice note, something like 'C'est un ragoût mexicain épicé à l'agneau. Le nôtre est mariné 24h avec des épices secrètes !' because it draws on its general knowledge and the business details.

Now the 'Tell us about your business' section has been highlighted with a ring. Within this section a tooltip transitions in to indicate that the user should try this next.

So the user clears the prefilled taco stand details and submits theirs. The hint text tells them the sort of things they should explain. They can submit text and images. They're encouraged to use the microphone button, that's transcribed live using ElevenLabs. The business details go into the system prompt. Now the user is encouraged to reset the WhatsApp demo and send some messages. They do so.

Now that the user has modified business details and sent some messages, the button to sign up is now highlighted. The user signs up using Clerk and the business details are saved to the DB.

Now they've signed up, they at `/admin`. They still have the mocked WhatsApp, but now they also have the option to pair their own WhatsApp phone number. (Note that now the user needs to press the button to generate the new pairing QR and it's only refreshed three times before they need to manually re-start.)

They can see all their Chalagente chats. By default Chalagente responds to all new conversations and continues to respond to conversations which it is already in. The user can change this setting. They can enable/disable for specific chats.
